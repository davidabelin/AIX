Operational Disclosure

Privacy

This page describes the current behavior of AIX Labs and its active lab arms. It is a narrow, project-specific description of the current stack and workflow, not a broad legal policy for unrelated services.

What the labs may store

AIX lab pages may store gameplay data, training-session records, model metadata, job status, and operational events needed to run the labs and inspect their results.

Cloud services

Deployed apps may use Google Cloud services configured for the project, including application hosting, storage, logs, secrets, and database-backed persistence where enabled.

What the labs are not

These labs are not currently presented as ad-supported consumer products. No account system or payment-processing flow is currently exposed in the AIX labs.

Retention and debugging

User-submitted or generated gameplay and training data may be retained for experimentation, debugging, model evaluation, or future model improvement within the active lab projects.

Questions or removal requests

If you have a privacy question or want to raise a removal request tied to current AIX lab data, use the Contact Us page and the linked GitHub issue trackers so the request lands in the correct project context.
